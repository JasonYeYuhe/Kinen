Kinen — Private Journal with On-Device AI

Your thoughts stay on your device. No servers. No third-party AI.

Kinen is a privacy-first journaling app with on-device AI that analyzes your mood, detects thought patterns, and helps you reflect deeper — without sending a single byte to any server.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Every word you write stays on your device. No accounts. No servers. No tracking. No data collection. Your thoughts belong to you alone. All AI analysis runs locally using Apple's NaturalLanguage framework. Kinen does not use OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or any third-party AI service — verify in-app any time at Settings → Privacy → Privacy Inspector.

ON-DEVICE AI THAT UNDERSTANDS YOU
- Automatic mood and sentiment analysis (Apple NaturalLanguage)
- CBT-based cognitive distortion detection (8 patterns, deterministic, local)
- Life theme discovery (work, health, relationships & more)
- Smart writing prompts based on your mood and history
- Weekly and monthly recaps generated on-device

8 GUIDED TEMPLATES
Daily Review, Gratitude Journal, Morning Pages, CBT Three-Column, Dream Journal, Goal Reflection, Weekly Review, and Free Write.

BEAUTIFUL INSIGHTS
- Mood trend charts (7, 30, 90 days)
- Sentiment analysis visualization
- Writing activity heatmap
- Year-at-a-glance calendar with mood colors
- Journaling streak tracking

VOICE JOURNALING
Speak your thoughts. On-device speech recognition transcribes in real time.

SYNC & BACKUP
- iCloud sync across all your Apple devices (Pro)
- AES-256 encrypted backups with your password
- Export to Markdown, JSON, or plain text

SECURITY
- Face ID / Touch ID app lock
- Crisis detection with mental health resources
- Medical disclaimer for CBT features

Kinen is a self-reflection tool, not a substitute for professional mental health care.
